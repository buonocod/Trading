Supplier

1. Introdution & Requirements

Below are the assets that can be produced by external suppliers, all using specific supplier templates, which are available to download below.

  • All creative must be with your Argos Digital Merchandising contact at least 10 days before it is due to go live.
  • If not your assets will likely be rejected for use on Argos.co.uk and our other digital channels.
  • Incorrectly provided assets will be rejected until correct as per the templates and guidelines.

Please supply both assets and formats as below:

  • JPEG (or PNG for ISDB and APPS)
  • Layer PSD (CS6 compatible)
  • 72dpi
  • RGB colour mode

Supplier Highlight Banner

Highlight banners are assets which sit at the top of any page across the argos.co.uk site. This gives customers better visibility of what promotions are live on a week-by-week basis. The advantage of having such an asset live, is that it's the first thing customers will see when browsing. Furthermore it's more prominent than the other assets we create and therefore allows more click throughs which in turn generates more sales. ASSET INFORMATION

 

  1. 1. Text Area - Text will be inputted by the in-house Argos Creative Team. The background image should not interfere with this text area and should allow white text to be legible.
  2. 1a. Header (Large Text) -
    Font - Argos Condensed Extra Bold
    Font Colour - White (#FFFFFF)
    Font Size - 40px
    Leading - 40px
    Tracking - 150
    Character Count - Maximum 35 chracters (inc. white space), maximum 2 lines
  3. 1b. Body (Small Text) -
    Font - Argos Book
    Font Colour - White (#FFFFFF)
    Font Size - 16px
    Leading - 18px
    Tracking - 60
    Character Count - Maximum 155 characters (inc. white space), maximum 3 lines.
  4. 2. Button - The position of the button is fixed but can say SHOP NOW or VEIW NOW depending on the content.
  5. 3. Background - The supplier can use the full width of the banner to apply their branding and styling. So long as this does not interfere with the text area on the left. No additional text should be added to the background.
  1. 4. Creative area - The logo, hero product(s) and the copyright information (optional) should sit within this area. This should not interfere with the text area on the left.
  2. 4a. Logo - The logo can go anywhere within the creative area. However to balance out the creative it is suggested to place the logo in the top right hand corner of the guide as illustrated above. Reason for this is that it gives more space to feature the hero product(s).
  3. 4b. Hero products - Use this area to showcase your product(s), as this will be your selling point. Make sure there's an equal balance with the content in this area i.e. the logo, your product(s) and copyright information (if applicable).
  4. 4c. Copyright information - This is optional. If your branding requires this, then it is best placed here.
  5. 5. Cut-off area - Ensure logos/products are not placed in this cut-off area (135px on the left and right) as they will not appear when viewed on tablets.

Supplier Search Banner

Search banners sit on any page where a customer has searched for a product; these are located at the top of a product list. Dimensions of these assets are 730 pixels by 200 pixels as illustrated below. Having an asset is beneficial as it is more prominent than having an editorial due to the space available. Furthermore, this will be the first thing customers see when they search for a product related to your industry i.e. toys or kitchen appliances and will appear above all the other searches. Please see below where they sit on the argos.co.uk site:

WHERE THEY SIT ON ARGOS.CO.UK



Please see below the dimensions and examples of search banners provided by our exisiting suppliers.

Width HEIGHT Optimum Max. Filesize (Jpeg)
730 px 200 px 100 KB

EXAMPLES

download psd template

ASSET INFORMATION




  1. 1. Creative Area - Use the entire canvas area to get across your message. Keep it clean and simple. If the background is a light colour, then please add a 1px border #CCCCCC. This is so that it doesn't bleed into the background of the argos.co.uk site.
  2. 2. No prices - Pricing information should not be within the banners, to avoid any legal discrepancies during the lifetime of the asset.
  1. 3. Copy - Limit the amount of copy within the creative where possible to keep the banner clear for the customer to absorb and scan quickly.
